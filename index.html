Hello world! I am Hailey, and I tell data-driven stories.

I recently graduated from Berkeley Graduate School of Journalism and am currently a data and graphics reporter at the Los Angeles Times. Previously, I was a reporter at Bloomberg, Lookout Santa Cruz, Oakland North, Rhythmns Monthly and Business Today.

I write, code and make maps. When I am not in front of my laptop, I can be found in a cinema or a local bookstore.
